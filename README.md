# Endgame Expanded

The datapack to lengthen your Minecraft playthrough!

This datapack adds:
- A new armor set above netherite!
- An overhauled enchanting system!
- Three new bosses, each with special weapons!

## Features

- The Wildfire. The Wildfire can be found in the nether. It has 400 HP, and the ability to summon blazes periodically. Defeating the Wildfire drops an item called the Infernal Crossbow. The Infernal Crossbow has a really fast load time, and turns regular arrows shot using this item into fireballs.


- The Storm. The Storm can be found in snowy biomes. It has 400 HP, and the ability to summon breezes periodically. Defeating the Storm drops an item called Rocket Boots. Rocket Boots allow you to fly. You can pick between one of two options for flight in the settings menu for Endgame Expanded (accessed under the pause menu): Lift, which lifts you up by holding Space, or Hover, which acts like creative flight.


- The Goliath Birdeater. The Goliath Birdeater can be found in spider caves, which can be found in the jungle. The Goliath Birdeater has two abilities: one that summons spiders, and another that shoots a cobweb projectile at the player. Defeating the Goliath Birdeater drops an item called the Silkwoven Bow. The Silkwoven Bow turns regular arrows shot using this item into cobweb projectiles.


- Infernal Armor and Tools. You can upgrade netherite with an upgrade template, a nether star, and your netherite item in a smithing table to a new tier.

### Enchantments

New Enchantments:

- Haste (Weapon): Increases attack speed.
- Smelt (Pickaxe): Smelts ores mined.
- Stun (Mace): Stuns smashed players for two seconds.

### Enchantment Overhaul

Enchantments have been split up into tiers: common, amythest, aquatic, and echo. Endgame Expanded also replaces the enchantment table with craftable enchanted books. Each tier of enchanted book has a different recipe, and grants a random enchantment from the tier's enchantments.

Recipes:
- Common: 1 book, 2 lapis lazuli
- Amythest: 1 book, 2 lapis lazuli, 1 amythest shard
- Aquatic: 1 book, 2 lapis lazuli, 1 prismarine shard
- Echo: 1 book, 2 lapis lazuli, 1 echo shard
